What is it?

Spectral Challenge is a call to makers worldwide to tackle real-world environmental problems with low-cost, open source spectrometry.

We're talking about the dream of taking a device you've constructed into your neighborhood and testing for heavy metals, oil contamination, or other toxics, without needing to have a PhD or knowing how to program .

Two Challenges

Stage 1: Learning

The goal of Stage 1 is to support and encourage the thousands of people building and using open source spectrometers by sharing your experiences learning about the process of open source spectrometry for the whole community.

Stage 2: Real World Use

The goals of Stage Two is to use a lowcost, open source spectrometer to identify environmental contaminants.
